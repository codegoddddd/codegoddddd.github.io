Temulawak



Overview

Temulawak, also known as Javanese ginger, is a plant that is native to Indonesia. It has a distinct, bitter taste and is used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of conditions, including digestive issues and respiratory infections.

Usage

Temulawak can be consumed as a tea or as part of a dish. It is often combined with other herbs to create a synergistic effect.

Research

Several studies have been conducted on the medicinal properties of temulawak. One study found that it has anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, which may make it useful in treating conditions such as acne and gum disease.
